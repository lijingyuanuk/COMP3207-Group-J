  • You will find all usefull tools in toolbelt below.
  • Click on a tool that you want to place on the maze field.
  • You can create land using Grass field and get rid of it using Marsh field.
  • You can always use a Bucket to add some speed to your work.
  • Grass is the only field that can be used to place items like gems , keys , potions , castle and adventurer .
  • You can erase all the items using Eraser .
  • Place Stamina Potions in strategic places, so your adventurers will be able to survive! Stamina Potions recover only 10 stamina and up to maximum 20.
  • Place some Gems around your adventure filed. Make your adventurers rich!
  • The Castle is the only refuge for the adventurers. Place it to help them survive the night.
  • Your adventurers will need all the Keys you are going to place on the maze field to enter the castle.
  • You can edit the size of your maze by changing the width (max 30) and height (max 30) below.
  • You can also change the time limit (maximum 120 seconds) for adventurers to explore your maze. Then it gets dark, and adventurers are afraid of the dark :(.
  • Choose a nice and unique name for your maze. It will be easier to recognize it amongst adventurers.
  • REMEMBER, You and ONLY YOU are responsible for your creations. You can easly make an uncompleatable, horrible, nightmarish adventure :(!
Good luck with your creations, young creator!
